Research

Māori Values in IT

I designed a poster and published a paper highlighting issues for Māori in IT which was accepted at the CITRENZ conference. The research was done using entirely secondary sources (mainly other papers and articles) and highlights the importance of input from Māori and awareness of Māori cultural values in IT projects. The poster is currently showcased at the Te Tuinga Whānau Trust.

Creative Repurposing of Accessible Technologies (CRAT)

I wrote a research paper with help from John Calder, Senior Lecturer of MIT, where I took a $38 Android phone and conducted a series of experiments to find the full potential of a cheap/old phone. I did gamepad control, motion detection, video streaming, GPS tracking and speech recognition and compared high-end devices with the cheap alternative to prove the point that repurposing cheap/old phones may be the next big thing when it comes to teaching and experimenting.
