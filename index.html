  • Beef Curry

    Ingredients:

    1. Beef- 1 kg (with bone)
    2. Bay Leaf x 2
    3. Cardommom x 8-10
    4. Cinnamon x 3
    5. Clove x 4
    6. Oil- 3/4 cup
    7. Onion x 1
    8. Salt- 1tbsp
    9. Chilli Powder- 1tbsp
    10. Turmeric Powder- 1tsp
    11. Special Curry Powder- 2tbsp
    12. Fresh Cumin Powder- 1tsp
    13. Potato x 1

  • Beef Curry

    Steps:

    1. Take a big non-stick pan and fry the chopped onion. The onion doesn't need to be finely chopped. Fry until only slightly brown.
    2. Add the whole masalas. Fry until the onions are fully brown.
    3. Add ginger and garlic paste, then stir.
    4. Add all the ground masalas except for the cumin. Add a little water and mix well.
    5. Add beef and stir until the masala gets mixed very well with the beef.
    6. Add 1/4 cup water and stir.
    7. When the water evaporates, again add 1/4 cup water and stir.
    8. In the same way add 2 cups of water and boil the meat.

  • Beef Curry

    Steps:

    1. After half an hour in medium heat, the meat should be cooked properly. Then add a big potato cut into big pieces and add 1 cup water.
    2. After 15-20 minutes add cumin. Cook for 5 minutes and serve hot.
    3. After half an hour in medium heat, the meat should be cooked properly. Then add a big potato cut into big pieces and add 1 cup water.
    4. After 15-20 minutes add cumin. Cook for 5 minutes and serve hot.

  • Beef Curry

    Tips:

    1. When adding cumin, don't stir too hard otherwise the potatos will break. Just gently mix it with the curry.
    2. I like extra salty and hot food. The amount of salt and chilli is really upto you. But at least add half of what I added. You wouldn't want your curry too bland.
    3. The special curry masala recipe is in the video and you can also find it in the recipe section.
    4. Serve with rice/paratha/roti.

  • Mullet Recipe

    Ingredients:

    1. 1 large Mullet
    2. Chilli Powder - 1 tblsp + 3tspn
    3. Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
    4. Coriander Powder - 1/2 tsp
    5. Black Pepper Powder - 1 tsp
    6. Salt - 1 1/4 tsp + 2 tsp
    7. Black Pepper Powder - 1 tsp
    8. Oil - 1/2 cup
    9. Chopped Onions - 2 cups
    10. Garlic paste - 1 tblsp
    11. Ginger paste - 1 1/2 tblsp
    12. 1/2 Cauliflower
    13. 1 Potato
    14. 10 Okra
    15. 10 Long Beans
    16. 1 handful Coriander
    17. Panch Phoron - 1 tspn

  • Mullet Recipe

    Steps:

    1. In a large bowl, mix the mullet and the powdered turmeric, 1tblspn chilli powder, powdered coriander, salt and pepper. Ensure the pieces are coated throughly with the spices.
    2. Take a big non-stick pan and heat the oil. Get the oil to a high temperature.
    3. After the oil is hot, place the fish pieces individually in the oil.
    4. After all the fish are in the pan, turn the heat down to about 3/4 and continue frying for about 4 minutes.
    5. When the fish have turned brown, take them out of the oil and place in a seperate dish.
    6. In the same pan, put the chopped onions in and fry them in the leftover oil.
    7. After the onions are fried slightly, Add the ginger and garlic.
    8. After frying the mixture for about a minute, add 3tspn chilli powder and 2 tspn salt. Continue frying for another minute.
    9. Add the potatoes, cauliflower, okra, long beans and fry on 3/4 heat for about 10 mins.
    10. Add 3 cups water. Cover and cook on high heat for another 10 minutes.

  • Mullet Recipe

    Steps:

    1. After the vegetables are cooked, add the pieces of fish in with 1 cup water. Stir carefully
    2. Cover and cook for 5-10 minutes.
    3. Turn the heat down and sprinkle the cumin on top. Stir in carefully and turn the heat off

  • Mullet Recipe

    Tips:

    1. When adding cumin, don't stir too hard otherwise the fish pieces will break. Gently mix it with the curry.
    2. Make sure the fish is fully submerged under the water when cooking. That way you get the best flavour infusion.
    3. When you add the fish into the vegetable curry, add the water from the bowl you kept the fried fish in. There is plenty of flavour in that bowl.
